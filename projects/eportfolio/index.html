e-Portfolio

e-Portfolio was built as a part of Bristol University's Software Project Engineering module, a year long effort which focused on the principles of group based software development. Our client, a lecturer at The University of Bristol, wished for a site where users could document their achievements from their time in university in more depth than would be allowed in a CV.
